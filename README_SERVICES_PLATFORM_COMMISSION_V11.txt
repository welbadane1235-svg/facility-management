HireX Services Platform Commission V11

Added Services tab behavior:
- Services tab opens a smart cinematic modal.
- Two service paths:
  1) يبحث عن خدمة: service providers offering services.
  2) يطلب خدمة: clients requesting a service.
- Clicking a service category opens a smart category modal.
- Category modal supports filters: all / يبحث عن خدمة / يطلب خدمة.
- Each service card has عرض.
- Details modal shows only non-empty fields.
- No phone numbers are shown for services.
- Contact is through platform only.
- Payment modal:
  - shows price
  - calculates platform commission
  - shows net amount for provider
  - simulates platform payment
- Commission is controlled by COMMISSION_RATE in JavaScript. Current default = 10%.
